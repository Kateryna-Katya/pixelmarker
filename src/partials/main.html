Triple Chili

It's basically a spicy slot game with some
ancient civilization vibes thrown in.

So you've got hot peppers, tropical fruit, and ancient pyramids all mashed together - sounds crazy but it actually works pretty well. They took regular
slot mechanics and gave it this
Aztec/Mayan makeover
that looks pretty sweet.

With 5x8 reels, there's constantly stuff going on - never a dull moment.

ABOUT THE
GAME

A Classic Experience with a Fiery Twist
They didn't reinvent the wheel here, just slapped on some Aztec graphics and called it a day. Inspired by Mayan and Aztec motifs, the game creates a world filled with symbolic imagery, glowing colors, and exciting visual effects.

It's a weird but cool combo - ancient pyramids mixed with chili peppers and tropical fruit. Shouldn't work, but it does.

THE WORLD OF TRIPLE CHILI

Inspired by Ancient Cultures

The developers really went all-in
on the ancient civilization theme.

Featured symbols include:

  • Flaming peppers
  • Corn
  • Strawberries
  • Lime
  • Tropical fruits
  • The legendary Pyramid Wild

The sound effects and animations
really bring it to life - you can almost
feel the heat from those peppers.

GAMEPLAY OVERVIEW

Built for Constant Action

Triple Chili features:

  • Built for Constant Action

  • 8 rows

  • 60 paylines

More reels means more ways to win, which is always nice. Things move pretty quick, and
there's always something popping off on the reels.

WILD SYMBOL

The Legendary Pyramid

The Pyramid Wild is the most important symbol in Triple Chili.

It's your standard wild symbol - fills in for other symbols when you need it to.

You'll know it when you see it - that pyramid wild is basically the star of the show and can really make your spin.
